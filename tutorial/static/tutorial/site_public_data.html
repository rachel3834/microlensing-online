Public Microlensing Datasets

A number of datasets containing microlensing events have been made publicly accessible, including the following, summarized in URL::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/1803.01003.pdf::NAME=Poleski and Yee (2018)::GROUP=public-data

The NASA Exoplanet Archive actively encourages authors to share lightcurve data for published microlensing events: URL::URL=https://exoplanetarchive.ipac.caltech.edu/index.html::NAME=NASA Exoplanet Archive::GROUP=public-data

KMTNet Survey Data

Photometry of microlensing events from 2015 (commissioning season, Kim et al. 2017) and 2016 data (K2/Campaign 9 footprint, Kim et al. 2018). These include 841 events and a further 266 candidate events. Additional datasets are likely to be made public also.
REF::AUTHORS=Kim et al.::YEAR=2017::JOURNAL=AJ::VOLUME=155::PAGE=76::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/1703.06883.pdf URL::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/1703.06883.pdf::NAME=Kim et al (2017)::GROUP=public-data
URL::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/1801.08166.pdf::NAME=Kim et al (2018)::GROUP=public-data

UKIRT

UKIRT surveyed 18×106 stars in the Galactic Bulge (including microlensing events) in 2015 and 2016, and aperture photometry light curves have been released for the entire catalog URL::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/1610.02039.pdf::NAME=Shvartzvald et al.(2018)::GROUP=public-data

URL::URL=https://exoplanetarchive.ipac.caltech.edu/docs/UKIRTMission.html::NAME=UKIRT Data Release::GROUP=public-data
REF::AUTHORS=Shvartzvald et al.::YEAR=2017::JOURNAL=AJ::VOLUME=153::PAGE=61::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/1610.02039.pdf

VVV

The VISTA Variables in the Via Lactea Survey in near infra-red passbands publicly released (Minniti et al. 2010) aperture photometry lightcurve data for all sources between 2010 and 2015.
URL::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/0912.1056.pdf::NAME=Minniti et al.(2010)::GROUP=public-data

URL::URL=http://archive.eso.org/cms/eso-archive-news/New_Data_Release_of_VVV_Photometric_Catalogues_via_the_ESO_Science_Archive_Facility.html::NAME=VVV Data Release::GROUP=public-data
REF::AUTHORS=Minniti et al.::YEAR=2010::JOURNAL=NewA::VOLUME=15::PAGE=433::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/0912.1056.pdf

Space-based Imaging Data

Time-series imaging data from the Spitzer Microlensing Program (2014--) and from K2/Campaign 9 (Henderson et al. 2016) are available from the NASA archives below.
REF::AUTHORS=Henderson et al.::YEAR=2016::JOURNAL=PASP::VOLUME=128::PAGE=124401::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/1512.09142.pdf URL::URL=https://archive.stsci.edu/k2/::NAME=K2 data in MAST::GROUP=public-data

URL::URL=https://irsa.ipac.caltech.edu/data/SPITZER/docs/spitzerdataarchives/::NAME=Spitzer data archive::GROUP=public-data

OGLE

OGLE-II catalog of 2 × 105 variable sources in the Galactic Plane were published by Wozniak et al.(2002) REF::AUTHORS=Wozniak et al::YEAR=2002::JOURNAL=AcA::VOLUME=52::PAGE=129::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/astro-ph/0201377.pdf and can be explored through the online archive:
URL::URL=http://ogledb.astrouw.edu.pl/~ogle/photdb/::NAME=OGLE Data Archive::GROUP=public-data

214 OGLE-II/III lightcurves were published in URL::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/0807.3884.pdf::NAME=Udalski et al.(2000)::GROUP=public-data REF::AUTHORS=Udalski et al::YEAR=2000::JOURNAL=AcA::VOLUME=50::PAGE=1::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/0807.3884.pdf

3718 OGLE-III lightcurves were published by URL::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/1405.3134.pdf::NAME=Wyrzykowski et al.(2015)::GROUP=public-data REF::AUTHORS=Wyrzykowski et al::YEAR=2015::JOURNAL=ApJS::VOLUME=216::PAGE=2::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/1405.3134.pdf

MOA

The lightcurves of 474 microlensing events were published by URL::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/1305.0186.pdf::NAME=Sumi et al.(2013)::GROUP=public-data REF::AUTHORS=Sumi et al::YEAR=2013::JOURNAL=ApJ::VOLUME=778::PAGE=150::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/1305.0186.pdf

MACHO

The lightcurves of 564 microlensing events from the MACHO Bulge survey were published in URL::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/astro-ph/0410341.pdf::NAME=Thomas et al.(2005)::GROUP=public-data REF::AUTHORS=Thomas et al::YEAR=2005::JOURNAL=ApJ::VOLUME=631::PAGE=906::URL=https://arxiv.org/pdf/astro-ph/0410341.pdf
